Origin Story

How It Started

01

In the early years of Ethereum, EF leader Alex Van de Sande (avsa) led a team called Mist ‚Äî focused on mass adoption by building Dapps that make Ethereum simple.

02

Fabian Vogelsteller was brought in as lead Dapp developer. Both avsa and Fabian were keenly interested in contract standards ‚Äî and in the Mist Browser and Ethereum Wallet as the entry point for the ecosystem.

03

On Nov 3, 2015, just before Devcon 1, the team released a feature allowing anyone to create tokens in the Ethereum Wallet ‚Äî the first token creation UI in the ecosystem.

04

Upon releasing it, Fabian created MistCoin and sent half the supply to avsa.

02 / 09
Significance

The Standard

01

MistCoin was the first in hundreds of coins created from its source code in the Ethereum Wallet. These were the first tokens ever to be heavily traded and used on Ethereum ‚Äî Fabian and avsa were handing MistCoin out on Reddit.

02

A few days later, avsa demoed the feature as a keynote speaker at Devcon 1. Fabian gave a dedicated talk on standardization.

03

16 days after creating MistCoin, Fabian published the original EIP-20 draft with Vitalik Buterin ‚Äî paving the way to ERC-20: Token Standard, which defines every standard token in existence today.

Today

Wrapped & Available

01

In 2022, avsa and Fabian were approached to help fund a group interested in historic NFTs. avsa donated hundreds of thousands of MistCoin for giveaways, and a wrapper was created to make MistCoin a standard ERC-20 tradeable on any DEX.

02

Fabian donated a large additional amount ‚Äî half placed for sale ‚Äî funding a full-range V2 pool locked for 100 years. Permanent, manipulation-resistant liquidity.

03

MistCoin ‚Äî created by the co-author of ERC-20, the first token deployed in the Ethereum Wallet ‚Äî is now available for all to collect. The provenance chain is complete and verifiable on-chain.
