CUSTOM TOKENS

Tokens are currencies and other fungibles built on the Ethereum platform. In order for wallets and accounts to watch for tokens and send them, you have to add their address to this list. You can create your own token by simply modifying this example of a custom token contract or learning more on Custom Token standard.
